Journey through the Northern Areas Mountains of Hunza

Lake Attabad, Hunza

Attabad Lake is one of the most breathtaking attractions located in Gilgit Baltistan. It's home to a number of ski resorts, summer outdoor recreation, and tourist attractions. Snow and skiing are a significant part of the area's reputation.

Check out all the Wildlife

As spawning season approaches the fish acquire a humpback and protuberant jaw. After spawning they die and their carcasses provide a feast for gatherings of Markhor, Himalayan brown bear, and Golden Eagles.

Pack Accordingly

One of most important things when it comes to traveling through the great outdoors is packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring layers of clothing
  2. Pack sunscreen
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Pack light
